1. Purpose

Purpose of this Policy is to have defined Car Lease entitlements and benefits for eligible employees.

2. Scope

This Policy is applicable to all permanent employees of HR Unit- Zuari Industries Limited (ZIL), Corporate Office, Gurgaon (ZIL-CO.GGN) for impact level 4C (Sr. Manager) & above.

3. Roles, Responsibility & Authority

Human Resource & Admin Department is responsible and accountable for effective implementation of this Policy throughout the organizations.

4. Reference

Department Manual - HR & Admin

5. Definitions & Abbreviations

ZIL      Zuari Industries Limited
HR       Human Resources

9. Operations & Procedures

9.1 Eligible and interested employees are required to apply & raise requisition to HR Dept. This policy will be applicable for new car or not older than 05 years.

9.2 The Company is responsible for paying agreed lease rentals as per defined matrix directly to the Individual or leasing company (hereinafter collectively referred as "Lessor") for the purpose of this policy. In all cases copies of the vehicle registration (RC) must be submitted. Insurance and maintenance will be the responsibility of the Lessor. Further, taxes applicable would be deducted as per laws in force, while making lease rental. The terms and conditions of the lease will be as determined by the company.

9.3 Reimbursement claims to be submitted quarterly to the respective department for Payment. A valid salary receipt is required to avail tax benefits towards driver salary.

9.4 Employees have no option to migrate from the existing Company Owned Car Policy to the Car Lease Policy.

9.5 It is the responsibility of employee to use leased car with utmost care, serve on lime, driver carefully following all traffic rules & keep all related documents (registration, insurance, road tax papers, PUC, etc.) in the car always for production. Any injury or collateral damage arising during the usage period shall be the responsibility and liability of the user employee. A duplicate copy of these documents is also maintained by Administration for records.

9.6 Taxability of perquisite value will be as per Income Tax Act 1961 based on cubic capacity of the car.

9.7 The employee will bear the GST in case claimed by the Lessor.

9.8 In case of theft/ damage, FIR to be lodged with the police station immediately and copy of the same is to be obtained and submitted to "Admin Department" within 24 hours.

10. Foreclosure

Premature exiting from the car lease policy by returning the car at any point of time has been provided under this policy. However, where premature exit from the policy is not at the behest of the company, the foreclosure charges/ expenses shall be borne by the employee in totality. Instances of premature exit not at the behest of the company are foreseen as, but not limited to the following:

a) Resignations.

b) Personal Reasons

c) Retirements (Including Voluntary Early Retirement).

d) Dismissals from service will be treated on the same footing as cases, which are not at the behalf of the company.
