DEPARTMENTAL GET-TOGETHER POLICY

Purpose:

To foster collaboration, cohesiveness, and mutual understanding among employees within and across departments, each department is encouraged to organize a Get-Together outside the office premises.

These gatherings aim to strengthen interpersonal relationships, promote teamwork, and enhance the overall organizational culture.

Scope and Applicability:

This policy is applicable to all staff of the Company working at the HR Unit – ZIL Corporate Office, Gurugram (ZIL-CO.GGN).

Policy:

1. Budget Allocation

  • A maximum budget of ₹1,500 (Rupees One Thousand only) per employee is allocated for the departmental get-together per financial year.
  • The amount is to be utilized exclusively for approved departmental gatherings within the same financial year.

2. Frequency

  • One annual get-together utilizing the full allocated amount, or
  • Two smaller gatherings, ensuring that the total expenditure does not exceed ₹1,500 per employee per financial year.
  • Carry forward of unused budget to the next financial year will not be permitted.

3. Participation & Structure

  • The get-together should encourage inter-departmental participation to promote collaboration beyond functional boundaries.
  • Events should ideally include participants from at least 4-5 departments.
  • Any employee may take the initiative to plan and coordinate the event, typically for a group of 10-15 participants.
  • Heads of smaller departments are encouraged to collaborate and club their teams to ensure inclusivity and active participation.

4. Planning & Communication

  • The venue and date of the event must be finalized in advance and communicated to all participants to ensure maximum attendance.
  • Teams are encouraged to integrate discussions on departmental strategies, action plans, or improvement ideas as part of the interaction, fostering both engagement and learning.
  • Activities may include informal discussions, team-building games, or brainstorming sessions related to departmental objectives.

5. Expenditure Guidelines

Eligible Expenses:

  • Only food and soft beverages are permissible.
  • Alcoholic beverages are strictly prohibited.

Approval & Settlement:

  • The final bill, duly certified by the Head of Department (HOD) and approved by the Functional Head, must be submitted to the Finance & Accounts Department for settlement.
  • A list of attendees must accompany the bill.
  • The HR & Admin Department shall maintain event records and forward verified bills to Finance for reimbursement.
  • All bills must be submitted and settled within 10 working days from the date of the event.

6. Policy Administration:

  • Corporate HR handles policy clarifications and interpretations.
  • Management reserves the right to revise this policy as needed.
